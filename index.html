Process

You’re 5 steps away from a new website...

Work with me
01

Discovery

To kick things off we start with a discovery session so we can learn more about your business and what you’re trying to achieve with your new website.

02

Wireframing

We’ll create the structure for the whole site in wireframes to establish the flow of the website and how we’ll present your product or service to best achieve your goals.

03

High-fidelity designs

Next, I’ll turn these wireframes into fully polished designs. At this point you’ll be able to see what the finished website will look like.

04

Development

Once you’re completely happy with the design I’ll kickstart coding your site, using a CMS will make it super easy to update.

05

Hosting & Deployment

Next, we handle hosting and deployment, ensuring your site is fast, secure, and live for the world to see.

06

Launch

Finally, we launch your site, making it live and accessible to your audience, ensuring everything runs smoothly

How long does a website take to build?

A typical project will take 4-8 weeks, and where it falls in that range depends on:
- Size of the website (i.e. how many pages)
- Complexity of design
- Speed of feedback &communication

How much does a website cost?

There’s a lot of factors that go into how much a website ends up costing, but I use standardised pricing so you know you’re always paying a fair price.
‍
Most new websites will fall into the ₹5-₹10k range.

All situations are different though, so get in touch and I can give you a  quote.
